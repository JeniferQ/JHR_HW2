PlayStation Story

Our legacy in gaming

PlayStation has revolutionized gaming since its debut in 1994, pushing the boundaries of technology, storytelling, and immersive experiences. From the original PlayStation to the latest cutting-edge consoles, Sony has consistently set new standards in gaming, delivering iconic franchises, groundbreaking hardware, and innovative features that have shaped the industry. With each generation, PlayStation has evolved, offering players richer graphics, deeper gameplay, and more connected experiences, making it a cornerstone of gaming culture worldwide.

Explore the evolution of PlayStation through the years as we take you on a journey across its legendary console lineup. From the revolutionary pioneer PS1 to the rise of virtual reality and cloud gaming, this timeline showcases our products that defined gaming history. Whether you're reliving nostalgic memories or discovering the legacy for the first time, the PlayStation story is a testament to the power of innovation and play.

PlayStation 3

"The Revolution"

The PlayStation 1 (PS1), released by Sony Computer Entertainment in 1994 in Japan and 1995 in North America and Europe, was the first console in the PlayStation lineup. It was developed after Sony's failed collaboration with Nintendo on a CD add-on for the Super Nintendo. As a result, Sony decided to create its own console, using CD-ROMs instead of cartridges, which allowed for larger games with more content and detailed graphics.
